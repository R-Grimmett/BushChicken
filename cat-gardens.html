Gardens

Want to see different plants?

Other gardens are a way to see many different plants! You can see so many that you don't have in your home. Many people have gardens you can visit, and sometimes you can also help them look after their plants!

Find a Garden Near You!

Jane Street Community Garden

Free to enter and right next to the local park! This garden is great to check out to see the work of other plant lovers in West End. Not only that, but you can help out too! Take your closest adult and head to one of their working bees to become a busy gardening bee.

For Adults:

All of these gardens are free to enter, but vary greatly in size. Most are also easy to get to, however, some might require more planning ahead of time!

As always, sun protection is strongly recommended before visiting any of these locations. We recommend checking the weather beforehand as well.
