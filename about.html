About Bush Chicken

Do you like plants and live near West End? At Bush Chicken we definitely do!

This website has a collection of plant-related activities and places to visit near West End, Brisbane. Some are free to experience, and sadly some are not.

For Adults:

This website has a mix of both free and paid activities and locations for any kid interested in plants or gardening. Unfortunately a lot of places in Brisbane related to plants are in busy locations, so Bush Chicken strongly recommends that at least 1 adult accompanies children visiting places mentioned.

Good outdoor safety will go a long way in protecting both you and your kids - remember hats and sunscreen.

About the Author:

G'Day!

My name's Rachael and I'm the author of Bush Chicken. Like you, I love plants and want to be able to share my plant finds with all of you!

I have been gardening for the past decade and have a small collection of pot plants in my place in West End. Some of my favourites are succulents and cacti.
