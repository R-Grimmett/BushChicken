Mappins

This store has an insane amount of plants!

While Mappins is a plant store, they also have their own collection to explore! You can see some unique succulents and vibrant tropical plants at the same time.

If you like fish, you can also get some plants for your fish friends here. Mappins also has a section where they sell fish and water plants.

They also offer workshops for you to make your own plant creations! Make sure to ask an adult if you can do one.
