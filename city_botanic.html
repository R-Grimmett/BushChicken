City Botanic Gardens

This garden is huge!

The City Botanic Garden is in the middle of Brisbane, and has a lot of different plants! You can explore in a tropical rainforest and end up surrounded by roses. You can see bamboo, or look at all of the fountains and sculptures they have.

The garden is run by the local council, and is free to enter. They also have many events and activities held there, so be sure to check out whats on!
